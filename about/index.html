About Me

Hey, I'm Gerry, a game designer with 10+ years of experience in mobile games. I'm all about creating fun, intuitive experiences that players actually enjoy.

Over the years, I've had the chance to lead multiple game projects from concept to launch, guiding teams through gameplay design, balancing, monetization, and live ops. I've also led a department of game designers, mentoring junior designers, shaping design processes, and helping align creative ideas with business goals. I love the full development cycle and finding that sweet spot between creativity and functionality.

Outside of work, I'm into retro games, movies, music, and all things pop culture. This site is where I share my projects, experiments, and whatever else I'm tinkering with.

Thanks for stopping by!
